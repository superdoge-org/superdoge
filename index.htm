About SuperDoge

SuperDoge launched in 2021 as the first charity-focused meme coin, parodying both Dogecoin and Superman. With a 6% transaction tax, 2% is burned, 2% goes to holders, and 2% was allocated to charities. Now community-driven, this fund will support development, marketing, listings, and future charity efforts.
