How to Buy SuperDoge ($SUPDOG)

Step 1: Set Up a Wallet

Download a wallet that supports Binance Smart Chain (BSC):

  • MetaMask Download here
  • TrustWallet Download here

Back up your recovery phrase. For MetaMask, add Binance Smart Chain:

  • Network Name: BNB Smart Chain
  • RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org
  • Chain ID: 56
  • Currency Symbol: BNB
  • Block Explorer: http://bscscan.com

Step 2: Fund Your Wallet with BNB

Buy BNB on Binance, Coinbase, or KuCoin, and send it to your wallet’s BEP-20 address (starts with “0x”). Use “Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20)” when withdrawing.

Keep a little extra BNB (0.01–0.05 BNB) for gas fees.

Step 3: Connect to PancakeSwap

Visit https://pancakeswap.finance and click “Connect Wallet.” Choose MetaMask or TrustWallet.

Make sure your network is set to BNB Smart Chain.

Step 4: Swap BNB for $SUPDOG

Go to “Trade” > “Swap”. Set BNB as the “From” token. Paste the SuperDoge contract below into the “To” token field:

0x622A1297057ea233287ce77bdBF2AB4E63609F23

Set slippage to 6–8% (click the ⚙️ icon). Increase to 10% if needed.

Click “Swap” and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

Step 5: Add $SUPDOG to Your Wallet

After the swap, manually add $SUPDOG if it doesn’t show up:

  • MetaMask: Add Token > Custom Token > paste contract
  • TrustWallet: Tap the toggle (top right), paste the contract

Step 6: Verify Your Purchase

Check your balance in your wallet, or visit BscScan and search your address.

Safety Tips

  • Always verify the contract address: 0x622A1297057ea233287ce77bdBF2AB4E63609F23
  • Beware of phishing sites: Use official PancakeSwap
  • Secure your wallet: Never share your recovery phrase or private keys
  • Research before investing: Crypto is volatile. Know the risks.
